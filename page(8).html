ExerciseS Reflection

Working with CSS properties like overflow, display, height, width, and position helped me understand how to control the layout of elements on a webpage. Using overflow taught me how to handle content that doesn’t fit in a container, while display helped me decide whether elements should appear as blocks, inline, or flex. Adjusting height and width made me realize how important sizing is for readability, and the position showed me how to place elements exactly where I want them. Adding a background image made the page more visually appealing. Experimenting with these properties made me feel more confident in designing web pages and controlling their structure.
