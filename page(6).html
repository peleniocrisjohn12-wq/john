Exercises Reflection

Creating this HTML form was a new experience for me, and I learned a lot about how forms work on a webpage. I used different input types like text, date, and radio buttons, which helped me understand how users can enter information. Designing the service agreement form made me realize how important forms are in real life for collecting accurate information. I also learned how to organize content using headings and tables to make it easier to read. Although some parts of my code could be improved, like fixing colspan and closing tags, I feel proud that I could make a functional form that collects details for both the service provider and client.
