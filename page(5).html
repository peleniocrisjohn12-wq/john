Exercise Reflection

Creating this HTML table helped me understand my daily routine better. By arranging each activity into specific time slots, I realized how busy and structured my day actually is. It showed me how much time I spend on chores, preparing for school, and even simple things like breakfast or using my phone. The table also made me more aware of how important time management is, especially as a student. Making this code was challenging at first because of the many rows, colors, and colspan or rowspan, but seeing the final output made me feel proud because it looked organized and meaningful.
